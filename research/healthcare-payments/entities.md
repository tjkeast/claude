# Key Entities — Healthcare Payments

Running reference of companies, products, and people in the space.

## Incumbents / rails
- **HICAPS** — NAB subsidiary; dominant AU health claiming + EFTPOS rail (est. 1998).
  Products: HICAPS terminals, HICAPS Digital, Medicare Easyclaim, wallet claiming.
- **Tyro Health** — ASX-listed (ASX:TYR) payments company; health vertical.
  Products: **HealthPoint** (claiming rail), **Medipass** (acquired from NAB
  Ventures for A$22.5M). Phased out HICAPS support 3 Feb 2023.
- **LanternPay** (Lantern Claims) — bank-agnostic health/NDIS/aged-care claims
  rail; used by CBA and ANZ. Being acquired by NAB/HICAPS (from InLoop).
- **DXC** — IT firm that built bank-agnostic health claims rails.
- **CommBank Health** — major-bank competitor; payment solutions for health pros.

## Private health funds (claim through these rails)
- Medibank, Bupa, nib, HCF, GU Health (wallet-claiming early adopters: Medibank,
  Bupa, nib, GU Health).

## Practice-management software (distribution layer)
- Best Practice (GP), Cliniko (+HealthPoint), Halaxy, Nookal, Genie/Gentu,
  Power Diary.

## Government / regulators
- Australian Govt Department of Health, Disability and Ageing — Medicare,
  "assignment of benefit" reform.
- (Watch: ACCC for M&A, RBA for card-payment regulation, APRA for PHI.)
